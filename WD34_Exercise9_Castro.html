About Us

Luk Yuen
Luk Yuen is a Chinese-themed restaurant that offers a casual ambiance, friendly and attentive crew and affordable, hearty Chinese dishes that will truly please your tastebuds.
Luk Yuen is known as a noodle house serving beef noodles, wanton noodles, and their signature King Dao noodles (spicy pork). Luk Yuen also serves variety of Chinese menu from dimsum to desserts. I highly recommend Beef Tendon, String Beans with Minced Pork, Raddish Cake (deep fried or pan fried), Dumplings with Chili Oil, Crispy Chicken, and Salt & Pepper Spareribs. Service is good, food is affordable.
